Trigger

a태그(href로 id값 전달) 
<a href="#modal1" class="btn is-grays-1" data-modal="open">a tag</a>
<button type="button" class="btn is-grays-1" data-modal="open" data-url="#modal1">button tag(data-url로 id값 전달)</button>
<button type="button" class="btn is-grays-1" onclick="customAlert.alert('타이틀', '텍스트', 'trueFn()');">alert</button>
<button type="button" class="btn is-grays-1" onclick="customAlert.confirm('타이틀', '텍스트', 'trueFn()', 'falseFn()');">confirm</button>
